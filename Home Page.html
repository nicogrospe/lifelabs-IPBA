For the first quarter, we conducted a survey with the three content types that we considered. The survey determined the content we were going to use for our youtube channel, who our target audience is, along with the needs we need to meet for content creator and audience.

Quarter two

At Blooming Wellness Hub, we believe that true wellness blooms from within. Our channel is dedicated to inspiring and guiding you on your journey to a healthier lifestyle. Through engaging discussions, insightful podcasts, and advice, we aim to empower you to take charge of your physical, mental, social, and spiritual health.

Quarter 3

Quarter 4

For our channel's first podcast episode, we chose to discuss personal puberty experiences, changes in our lifestyle throughout the pandemic, and offer advice on how to grow and develop. These themes will raise awareness and guide our viewers as they go through their own experiences.
